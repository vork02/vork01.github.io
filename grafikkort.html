Grapics Card

A grapics card is the part of the pc that display your desktop, the better graphics card you have the better grapic you get. Many games are also based on the graphis card, that means the better graphics card, the bigger game can you run.

There is also a big price difference between the grapics cards, you can get a low end graphics card that can run little games for about 600DKK - 800DKK. And the high end graphics card can cost you about, 6000DKK to 10000DKK.
