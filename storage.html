Storage

The storage is the the place the computer store everything. The storage is the part of the pc that remembers everything. It's the place where your games are, your work files or something completely different. Everything you have on your computer is in there.

Storage can be expensive, but that depends on wich one you choose to buy. HardDrives are usually cheaper that Solid State Drives, but solid state drives are much faster. A harddrive with 500 gb, can easlly cost 300DKK, where an ssd can cost up to 1000DKK for one ssd. SSD is a shortcut for Solid State Drive, and HDD is a shortcut for HardDrive
