Power Supply

There isn't mush to say about the power supply. The power supplys job is to power the pc, and make sure that it gets enough power for the whole pc.

You can get different power supplys with different wattage, the more wattage the higher price. You use much wattage for a big pc, fx. with 4 fans and 2 grapic card and so on.
